## Comment Response Strategy

Reply to EVERY comment. Even short ones. Guidelines:
- Questions about features: Give a direct answer + link to the relevant page
- "Cool project" type comments: Ask them what platform they'd use it for
- Critical comments: Thank them, acknowledge the point, explain the roadmap
- Competitor comparisons: "We're actually free for [X] - check out socialmate.studio/vs/[competitor]"
- First-time founders asking questions: Be generous, share the build-in-public story
